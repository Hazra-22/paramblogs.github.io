Why Quantum Physics?

Every STEM fellow is familiar with the term "Quantum" though he might be barely knowing the details. It all started in early 1900s when the good old Planck had trouble in understanding his equation. Many great men scratched their heads. But it was Einstein and Bose from India who could give some satisfactory answers. Later Heisenberg and his contemporary scientists taught us how to formally study this thing.
